Dead, Not Helpless
No body, no weapons ‚Äî but you can possess every object in the dungeon. Furniture, traps, mechanisms, light sources, even fragments of your old legendary gear. The dungeon itself is your weapon. Every room is a puzzle of what to possess and when.

Up, Not Down
Every dungeon crawler goes deeper. You go up. The inversion changes everything: deeper floors are easier (recently cleared), upper floors are a nightmare (years overgrown). Each Kenmi biome pack maps to a floor. The surface is the final boss.
